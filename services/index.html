Services

CONSULTATION

Free, No Obligation Consultation, Design & Quotation
A project starts with an initial consultation. Each specification is unique, and our Project Managers will discuss your plans, objectives and any services your organisation requires. We do not assume anything and with a wealth of knowledge and experience, we will find out exactly what you want to achieve, and work out the best way to get you there.From consultation, we will present a speculative design and quotation.

 

STAND DESIGN

A DESIGN TEAM PRODUCING CUTTING EDGE CONCEPTS TO YOUR REQUIREMENTS
Stumps has a team of professional designers that bring creative & innovative design solutions to our clients.We discuss every element of the exhibition, quotation and design with our potential clients – making any changes if necessary – ensuring you are completely satisfied before commissioning us with your project.
By choosing one of our well-designed concepts, your organisation will stand out above the numerous non-descript offerings at a show.

STAND BUILD

THE COMPLETION TIME IS OF CRUCIAL IMPORTANCE.
Project completion time is crucial. Key to delivering multiple stands on time is organised project management. Our team will organise the many elements of your project, whilst ensuring your build receives the time required for a first-class construction, installing your stand to exacting standards on site.
We have our own production workshop to build & pre-build your exhibition stand, meaning, If required we can store, redecorate and reinstall at your next show or event.

 

PROJECT MANAGEMENT

Exhibition Stand Planning & Project Management.
Want to focus on the actual event and not get overwhelmed with administration work, required to make everything happen? Leave it to professionals, We provide a dedicated project manager from start to finish, making the whole process easy throughout.We provide stress free service to our customers. Stumps has established relationships with venues, show organisers and event services providers. We will deal with all technical documentation, approvals and make sure you get what you want.

LOGISTICAL SUPPORT

Our Own Logistics fleet:
Ensuring build components and your product arrives on time and in pristine condition, is of the highest priorities for our team. Unlike many competitors, we operate our own fleet of vehicles of varying size and capabilities to ensure your project is delivered anywhere throughout delhi and covering 500 KMS.

 

SHIPPING & STORAGE

SAFE AND SECURE STORAGE FACILITY
Short on space? Let us take the strain out of your display or stand shipping and storage.
We also operate a storage facility in our warehouse, offering safe and secure storage of your stand and/or product for use at future shows.

 
 
 
 

EXHIBITION ESSENTIALS:

Furniture, graphics, audio-visual, showcases & Pull up banners – we’ve got these essentials covered for your stand too.

Exhibition Furniture

Often overlooked, great furniture is vital to look of your stand as well as the comfort of you and your guests. We have a huge selection to choose from whether you’re looking for high stools to comfortable sofa sets , chairs & tables of different styles.

Artwork & Graphics:

A State-of-the-art Graphic Studio Equipped With Latest Printing Machines & Designers That Can Design, Interpret And Manipulate Clients’ Ideas To Create Spectacular Visual Images.

The studio can offer advice on design and processing, with the installation team ensuring expert fitting on site. The studio is equally able to design and print stunning pop-ups and banner stands to meet any portable display requirements.

Audio Visual & IT

From the latest lighting to sound systems, truss systems, Chain hoists, amazing seamless video walls, P3 & P2 LED video walls, Touchscreens, 40 – 65’’ LED TV’S, iPads etc we can provide, install and manage all your on-stand AV-IT needs.

Staffing, Catering & Merchandise

Let us take care of those tricky finishing touches from finding the right professional promotional and stand staff, refreshments and catering, and the best branded merchandise and giveaways.
